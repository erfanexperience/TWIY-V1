TWIY Health Case Intake Platform

TWIY Health provides healthcare case intake coordination services designed to help individuals and organizations organize, document, and route healthcare-related cases efficiently. When you reach out to us, we may send transactional SMS updates that relate directly to your active case intake requests. We do not send marketing or promotional text messages.

How We Use SMS Communications

Our text messages are used only to support your initiated healthcare case intake interactions, including confirming receipt of your request, clarifying intake details, and coordinating next steps such as appointments or documentation. All SMS communications are transactional, limited in scope, and never used for advertising or broad marketing campaigns.

Our Commitment to Privacy and Compliance

TWIY Health is committed to protecting the privacy of individuals who contact us and to complying with applicable U.S. regulations and carrier requirements related to SMS communications, including A2P 10DLC guidelines. The information we collect is limited to what is needed to respond to your inquiries, process healthcare case intake requests, and keep you updated on the status of your case.

Our Privacy Policy describes in detail what information we collect, how it is used, and the safeguards we use to protect it. Our Terms & Conditions explain how our services work and the terms that apply to SMS communications, including opt-in, opt-out, and message and data rate disclosures.
